The Fidget Spinner is an extremely smooth 3-way bearing spinner. The three bearings on the sides act like weights to provide a smooth and elegant glide when spinning. These have center caps over the bearings which make it easier for people with bigger fingers to use. They also have the capability to spin on tables and desks.
